Lemon Tiramisu

Description

Lemon tiramisu is a delightful variation of the traditional Italian dessert, infused with the bright and tangy flavors of lemon. It combines the creamy mascarpone cheese with the tartness of lemon for a refreshing and indulgent treat.

Ingredients :

For the lemon syrup :

  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

For the mascarpone cream :

  • 16 ounces mascarpone cheese, softened

Source icon :

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream, whipped to soft peaks

For assembly :

  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Additional lemon zest for garnish

Steps :

  1. Make the lemon syrup : In a small saucepan, combine lemon juice and sugar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. Let cool completely. Source icon
  2. Prepare the mascarpone cream: In a large bowl, beat mascarpone cheese and powdered sugar until smooth. Gently fold in lemon zest and whipped cream.
  3. Assemble the tiramisu: Dip ladyfingers briefly in lemon syrup, and arrange them in a single layer in a glass baking dish. Spread a layer of mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mascarpone cream.
  4. Chill and serve: Cover the tiramisu with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight. 1 Before serving, sprinkle with additional lemon zest.
