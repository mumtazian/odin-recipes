American Lasagna

Description :

American lasagna is a beloved, hearty dish known for its rich flavors and comforting nature. It's a popular Italian-American adaptation that differs slightly from its traditional Italian counterpart.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce :

  • pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the Cheese Mixture :

  • ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper

Other Ingredients :

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Optional: additional Parmesan cheese for topping

Steps :

  1. Make the Meat Sauce :

    2. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Drain any excess fat.

    Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.

    Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, brown sugar, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.

    Bring to a simmer, reduce heat, and let simmer for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for a richer flavor.

    Prepare the Cheese Mixture :

    In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.

  2. Assemble the Lasagna :

    3. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

    Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.

    Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.

    Layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat sauce, repeating until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce.

    Sprinkle the top with mozzarella cheese.

    Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.

    Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly.

    Let cool for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.

  3. Optional: Sprinkle additional Parmesan cheese on top before serving for extra flavor.
